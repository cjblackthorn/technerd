# Privacy Policy

TechNerd Consulting respects privacy and aims to collect only information reasonably necessary for communication, consultation requests, scheduling, and project coordination.

## Information Usage

Information submitted through email or consultation requests may be used for:

- communication
- scheduling
- project evaluation
- service delivery

## Third-Party Services

TechNerd Consulting may use third-party services including scheduling, email, cloud storage, or communication platforms.

## Limitations

Electronic communication and third-party services cannot be guaranteed completely secure.
