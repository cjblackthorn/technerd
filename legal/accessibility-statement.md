# Accessibility Statement

TechNerd Consulting is committed to building and maintaining digital spaces that are accessible, usable, and respectful of real human needs.

## Our Goal

The goal of this site is to support clear navigation, readable content, keyboard access, responsive layouts, and compatibility with assistive technology where reasonably possible.

## Ongoing Improvement

Accessibility is an ongoing process. As the site develops, TechNerd Consulting will continue improving structure, contrast, readability, navigation, and usability.
