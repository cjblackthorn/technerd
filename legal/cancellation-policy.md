# Cancellation Policy

Consultation scheduling and project timelines depend on availability, workload, and project scope.

## Scheduling

Scheduled meetings or consultations may require advance notice for cancellation or rescheduling.

## Delays

Project timelines may shift because of scope changes, communication delays, vendor issues, technical limitations, or outside dependencies.

## Availability

TechNerd Consulting reserves the right to decline, pause, or discontinue work that falls outside agreed scope or operational capacity.
