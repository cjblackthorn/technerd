# Terms

This page outlines general expectations for TechNerd Consulting website use and consulting requests.

## Scope

TechNerd Consulting provides technology consulting, guidance, recommendations, and support within agreed project scope.

## Client Role

Clients should provide accurate information, keep appropriate backups, manage internal decisions, and approve changes before implementation.

## Service Limits

Recommendations are based on available information at the time of review. Technology environments, vendors, platforms, and risks may change over time.

## Outside Platforms

Some work may involve outside tools, services, vendors, or platforms. Their availability, policies, pricing, and behavior are outside TechNerd Consulting control.

## Updates

This page may be updated as services, workflows, and business operations develop.
