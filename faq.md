# Frequently Asked Questions

## What services does TechNerd Consulting provide?

TechNerd Consulting provides support across IT infrastructure, cybersecurity guidance, accessibility consulting, systems operations, and workflow optimization.

## Does TechNerd Consulting provide legal or compliance certification?

No. TechNerd Consulting provides guidance, recommendations, and consulting support but does not provide legal advice or compliance certification.

## Does TechNerd Consulting guarantee security or accessibility compliance?

No technology system can be guaranteed completely secure or fully compliant in all environments. Recommendations are provided on a best-effort basis.

## Is remote consulting available?

Yes. Many services can be provided remotely depending on scope and technical requirements.
