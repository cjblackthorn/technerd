# Security and Privacy

TechNerd Consulting approaches technology with a security-minded and privacy-aware philosophy grounded in practical operations, identity and access hygiene, recovery planning, and sustainable risk reduction.

## Areas of Focus

- account security
- identity and access governance
- multi-factor authentication
- password management
- admin account review
- access review cleanup
- account recovery planning
- backup and continuity planning
- phishing awareness
- privacy-conscious workflows
- physical and digital hardening
- audit-informed documentation
- sustainable security practices

## Realistic Security

Security is an ongoing process rather than a permanent state. Recommendations are intended to improve resilience, usability, access clarity, recoverability, and operational sustainability.
