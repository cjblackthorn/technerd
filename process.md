# Process

TechNerd Consulting uses a structured consulting workflow designed to clarify scope, improve communication, and support sustainable project planning.

## Request

Initial contact and project inquiry.

## Discovery Review

Assessment of project fit, scope, goals, accessibility considerations, and technical requirements.

## Consultation

Discussion of needs, systems, workflows, risks, accessibility concerns, and operational goals.

## Proposal and Scope

Definition of deliverables, limitations, timelines, responsibilities, and pricing.

## Engagement

Implementation, consulting, support, or advisory work begins after scope and expectations are agreed upon.
