Use this checklist to shape access review planning:

- Define review scope and systems.
- Confirm business and technical owners.
- Validate reviewer lists before launch.
- Document review criteria.
- Track approvals, removals, exceptions, and escalations.
- Retain evidence in a repeatable format.
- Confirm remediation after review completion.
