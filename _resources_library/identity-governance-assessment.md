Use these prompts to assess identity governance maturity:

- Are joiner, mover, and leaver processes documented?
- Are system owners accountable for access?
- Are privileged accounts reviewed differently from standard access?
- Are dormant and shared accounts identified?
- Are access review decisions evidenced?
- Are exceptions approved and periodically revisited?
- Are lifecycle failures monitored and remediated?
