# Who We Help

TechNerd Consulting is designed for people and organizations who want practical, accessible, sustainable technology support grounded in real operational experience.

## Common Clients

- small businesses
- solo operators
- nonprofits
- disabled professionals
- accessibility-conscious organizations
- teams with messy accounts, permissions, or access review needs
- people recovering from account, endpoint, or security disruption
- organizations needing practical systems, documentation, or workflow guidance

## Approach

TechNerd Consulting focuses on realistic, sustainable improvements rather than fear-driven technology messaging.

The goal is to help people build systems that are usable, maintainable, accessible, recoverable, and secure enough for real-world use.
