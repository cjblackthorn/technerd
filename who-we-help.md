# Who We Help

TechNerd Consulting is designed for people and organizations who want practical, accessible, sustainable technology support.

## Common Clients

- small businesses
- solo operators
- nonprofits
- disabled professionals
- accessibility-conscious organizations
- overwhelmed technology users
- organizations needing practical systems guidance

## Approach

TechNerd Consulting focuses on realistic, sustainable improvements rather than fear-driven technology messaging.

The goal is to help people build systems that are usable, maintainable, accessible, and secure enough for real-world use.
