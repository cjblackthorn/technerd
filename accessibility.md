# Accessibility

Accessibility is part of how TechNerd Consulting approaches technology, systems, and infrastructure.

Technology should reduce barriers, not create them.

## Accessibility Philosophy

TechNerd Consulting believes accessible systems are better systems. Accessibility improves usability, sustainability, maintainability, and real-world human interaction with technology.

## Areas of Focus

- Digital accessibility guidance
- Workflow accessibility
- Adaptive technology recommendations
- Accessibility-aware systems consulting
- Usability-focused technology support
- Accessibility-conscious design recommendations

## Commitment

TechNerd Consulting aims to support accessible, usable, and human-centered technology experiences wherever reasonably possible.
