Smaller organizations still need real security, but they rarely need enterprise ceremony. TechNerd applies enterprise security principles in a right-sized way: clear, practical, maintainable, and proportionate to the organization.

This path can include security health checks, account and permission reviews, Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace reviews, starter policies, incident planning, continuity planning, and practical systems governance.
